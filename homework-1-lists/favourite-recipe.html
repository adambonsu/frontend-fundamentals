Poached Egg with Bacon and Mushrooms on toast

  1. Ingredients

    • Egg(s)
    • Brioche Bread, sliced
    • Fresh mushrooms
    • Bacon, diced
    • Butter, a knob
    • White wine vinegar
    • Sea Salt
    • Ground Black Pepper

  2. Instructions

    1. Fry the bacon until golden brown
    2. Add the mushrooms and butter; allow to fry until the mushrooms are cooked through
    3. Drain mushrooms and bacon on a plate covered with kitchen towel
    4. Boil water in a saucepan
    5. Season the boiling water with salt and vinegar
    6. Mix the water into a cyclone and place the egg into the water
    7. After about 3 minutes, drain the egg on a plate covered with kitchen towel
    8. Toast the bread on both sides
    9. Cover one side of the toast with mushrooms and bacon
    10. Place the poached egg on top
    11. Enjoy
